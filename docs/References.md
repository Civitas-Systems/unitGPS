# References

Authoritative sources behind UnitGPS's data and methods. Cited in [[METHODOLOGY]] and
verified in [[QA_NOTES]].

## Standards & reference values

- **NIST Special Publication 811** (2008 ed.) — *Guide for the Use of the International
  System of Units (SI)*. National Institute of Standards and Technology. Unit-conversion
  constants (e.g. IT BTU = 1055.05585 J).
- **BIPM** — *The International System of Units (SI Brochure)*, 9th ed. (2019). Bureau
  International des Poids et Mesures.

## Greenhouse-gas metrics

- **IPCC AR4** (2007) — *Climate Change 2007: The Physical Science Basis*, WG I, Ch. 2,
  Table 2.14 (GWP values).
- **IPCC AR5** (2013) — *Climate Change 2013: The Physical Science Basis*, WG I, Ch. 8,
  Table 8.7 (GWP values).
- **IPCC AR6** (2021) — *Climate Change 2021: The Physical Science Basis*, WG I, Ch. 7,
  Table 7.15 and Supplementary Material (GWP values; CH₄ fossil 29.8 / non-fossil 27.0).

## Emission factors

- **U.S. EPA — GHG Emission Factors Hub** — stationary-combustion and other emission
  factors (e.g. anthracite: 103.69 kg CO₂, 11 g CH₄, 1.6 g N₂O per mmBTU).
- **U.S. EPA — eGRID** (Emissions & Generation Resource Integrated Database) — regional
  electricity emission factors.

## Software

- **NetworkX** — Hagberg, Schult & Swart (2008), *Exploring network structure, dynamics,
  and function using NetworkX*. Graph model and shortest-path routing.
- **pandas**, **Streamlit**, **Plotly**, **Matplotlib** — data handling and UI.
